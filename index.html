Step 2

Panvel / Mumbai → Pachad

🚉 From Panvel Station

  • Auto / Taxi to Panvel ST Stand (5–10 mins)
  • Take MSRTC Bus / Shared Jeep towards Panvel → Pen → Mangaon → Mahad → Pachad
  • Travel Time: 3–4 Hours
  • Bus Fare: ₹150 – ₹250
  • Shared Jeep: ₹250 – ₹350

🏛️ From Mumbai (CSMT / LTT)

  • Take MSRTC Bus to Mahad (Direct or via Pen)
  • Travel Time: 4–5 Hours
  • Fare: ₹250 – ₹350
  • From Mahad ST Stand: Bus / Jeep to Pachad (30–45 mins)
  • Fare: ₹40 – ₹60

Travel Info

Best Time & Travel Tips

📅 Best Time to Visit

Monsoon (Jun–Sep)
Lush greenery & waterfalls — most scenic
Winter (Oct–Feb)
Pleasant weather, ideal for trekking
Summer (Mar–May)
Hot — carry sufficient water

💡 Travel Tips

Start early from Panvel / Mumbai
Keep cash — ATMs not available at Pachad
Carry water, light food & raincoat (monsoon)
Ropeway can be crowded on weekends
Respect the fort & keep it clean
